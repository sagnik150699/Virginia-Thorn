Voice Over

Emmy Award-winning sound editor. Warm, rich & smooth voice from her professional home studio.

Virginia is an Emmy Award winning Sound Editor, a Meisner Trained Actor & a Classically Trained singer. She was recognised with a Prime Time Emmy for outstanding editing on the HBO David Simon show Generation Kill.

Her voice has been described as warm, rich and smooth. Her mother is from Argentina, her father is British RP. She grew up in South East London & her native accent is Estuary English. All projects are voiced, mixed and mastered from her professional home studio.
