Pocket Of Light is now funded by Arts Council England

The year kicked off with the fantastic news that our funding application to Arts Council England on behalf of Pocket Of Light, was successful.

The funded project, titled 'The Untitled Doug Lucie Play,' represents significant progress for our theatre company. Pocket Of Light Theatre was established in 2023 and mounted a successful production of John Patrick Shanley's 'Savage In Limbo' in 2024. I serve as Creative Producer for this endeavour, collaborating with Director Sebastien Blanc.

The production centres on the marginalisation of older voices in theatre and society, and features work by acclaimed playwright Doug Lucie, whose previous productions have appeared at prestigious venues including The Royal Court and The National Theatre.

Development has included research and development sessions at Arcola Theatre, supported by collaborator Mehmet Ergen. The full production is scheduled for 2027.
