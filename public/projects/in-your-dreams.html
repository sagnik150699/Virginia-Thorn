'In Your Dreams' with Directors Cut Theatre

Another great show with Directors Cut Theatre, showcasing new original work by professional Actors, Writers & Directors. The inspiration point for this show was 'In Your Dreams.'

I played Tara in Cerys Evans' 'Spark Joy,' a piece exploring interpersonal dynamics where Tara attempts to persuade a transgender friend named Beth about a significant life proposal. Writer Evans made Tara the mouth piece for many of the well meaning & insensitive judgements & assumptions she has encountered as a trans woman. The production was directed by Andy Mcleod.
